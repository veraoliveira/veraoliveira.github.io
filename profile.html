Dr. Vera graduated as a dentist from University Egas Moniz, Portugal, in a Master program. She worked in the Dental University Hospital and some privates hospital clinics in areas including dental emergencies, oral surgery, special care dentistry and periodontology.

Following this she started her postgraduate training in Center of Portugal, Leiria, in private practice developing skills in cosmetic, implants dentistry, prosthodontic rehabilitation, TMJ disorders. She finished her postgraduate with honours.

Dr. Vera is very passionate about her work and does everything she can to take care of her patients. She believes that in order to continue providing the best care for her patients that we need to be able to stay up to date with the latest techniques.

Her ability to instil confidence in her patients with her calm manner and extensive knowledge has made her a firm favourite with some of the most nervous patients.
